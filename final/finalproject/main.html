What is Fliptop Battle League?

FlipTop is the largest and most recognized rap battle league in the Philippines, elevating hip-hop culture and showcasing incredible talent. FlipTop is largely inspired by the original rap battle leagues in the West that were established in 2008, such as Grind Time Now in the US, King of the Dot in Canada, and Don't Flop in the UK. These leagues served as a model for the creation of FlipTop and various other battle leagues globally.[1]
