Explore the legends and battles that defined FlipTop.

Notable Battles

  • FlipTop - Loonie/Abra vs. Shehyee/Smugglaz
    This epic battle from the Dos Por Dos Tournament showcased teamwork, wit, and lyrical excellence. It's a must-watch for fans of collaborative rap battles.
  • FlipTop - Shehyee vs. Sinio
    A fan-favorite battle known for its comedic brilliance and iconic rebuttals.
  • FlipTop - Shernan vs. Hazky
    This intense face-off highlighted raw energy and powerful delivery, cementing both emcees as crowd favorites. This is one of the funiest rap battles.
  • FlipTop - Loonie vs. Aklas
    A highly anticipated battle that featured thought-provoking lyrics and unforgettable punchlines, earning its place in FlipTop history.
